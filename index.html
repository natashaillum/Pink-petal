Services we provide

Fittings are by appointments only.

We focus on combining functionality with style, offering a range of options from practical essentials to beautifully designed pieces

Mastectomy prosthesis foam is a specialized material used to create a natural, comfortable shape inside a post-mastectomy bra or swimsuit. The lightweight foam is designed to mimic the look and feel of a natural breast form for women who have undergone mastectomy surgery.

The silicone gel and elastomer shell are formulated to mimic the look and feel of natural breast tissue.Silicone prostheses are lightweight, seamless artificial breasts designed for women who have undergone mastectomy surgery.

Mastectomy supplies encompass a range of products used by women who have undergone mastectomy surgery for breast cancer treatment. Key mastectomy supplies include prostheses, bras, swimwear, sleeves, and wraps designed specifically for a post-operative chest.

Compression sleeves are an important mastectomy supply used by many breast cancer survivors. Compression sleeves are worn on the arms to help manage lymphedema, a common condition after lymph nodes are removed during mastectomy surgery.

Mastectomy camisoles feature soft, breathable fabrics that do not irritate surgical scars and sensitive skin. They are designed with pockets or openings to accommodate drains during early stages of healing.

In the days and weeks following mastectomy surgery, there are several important aspects of care to manage recovery. Bandages and drainage tubes need regular maintenance to prevent infection while also monitoring for complications. Pain management is critical, often involving medications and restricted arm mobility. Assistance may be required for daily tasks like getting dressed. Special post-surgical bras and prosthetics help protect and stabilize the chest.
